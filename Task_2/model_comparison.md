# Model comparison results

We compared the accuracy of a Support Vector Machine and a Multi-Layer Perception model when given the task of classifying RGB images with their NDVI counterparts. After training and evaluation, the accuracies the models were as such:

- __SVM__: 68%
- __MLP__ ran on 20 epochs: 74%

SVM has proven to be the least accurate model when classifying processed images. However, both models got low accuracy scores.
